# Universities

## ITMO University

**Full Name:** Saint Petersburg National Research University of Information Technologies, Mechanics and Optics

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| Founded | 1900 |
| Location | Saint Petersburg, Russia |
| Type | Public Research University |
| Students | 15,000+ |

### Key Highlights

- **ACM-ICPC World Programming Championship** — holds the record for most gold medals (7 world champion titles)
- Renowned for **Computer Science**, **Artificial Intelligence**, **Optics**, and **Robotics**
- One of the key universities in Russia's **Project 5-100** initiative (aimed at reaching world top 100)
- Ranks highly in **QS World Rankings** for Computer Science

### Strong Programs

- Computer Science & Artificial Intelligence
- Information Security
- Photonics & Optics
- Robotics & Automation
- Mathematics
